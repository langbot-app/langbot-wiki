## Introduction

[OpenClaw](https://github.com/dingxiang-me/OpenClaw-Wechat) is an open-source AI Agent framework. Through the OpenClaw WeChat adapter, you can connect LangBot to your personal WeChat account, with support for group chats, streaming output, whitelist control, and more.

## Prerequisites

- LangBot is deployed and running

<Warning>
Please make sure your mobile WeChat version is the latest **8.0.69** or above, otherwise the OpenClaw WeChat adapter may not work properly. You can check your version in WeChat under "Me" -> "Settings" -> "About WeChat".
</Warning>

## Configure in LangBot

### 1. Create Adapter

Launch the LangBot main program, create a new bot, fill in the bot name, select `OpenClaw 微信` as the platform/adapter, and click Submit.

![Create WeChat Adapter](/images/zh/deploy/bots/wexin/wexin_01.png)

### 2. Enable Adapter

Enable the adapter and save the configuration.

![Enable Adapter](/images/zh/deploy/bots/wexin/wexin_02.png)

### 3. Scan QR Code to Log In

Open the logs and check the log entry with an image icon to get the login QR code.

![Open Logs to View QR Code](/images/zh/deploy/bots/wexin/wexin_03.png)

Use your mobile WeChat to scan the QR code to complete the login.

<Warning>
Before scanning, please confirm again that your WeChat version is the latest **8.0.69** or above, otherwise the scan may fail.
</Warning>

![Scan QR Code](/images/zh/deploy/bots/wexin/wexin_04.jpg)

### 4. Confirm Successful Login

After scanning successfully, your phone will enter the ClawBot chat page on WeChat.

![WeChat ClawBot Chat Page](/images/zh/deploy/bots/wexin/wexin_05.jpg)

At the same time, the LangBot logs will show a successful connection message, and the token and account fields on the configuration page will be automatically updated, indicating a successful login.

![Log Information](/images/zh/deploy/bots/wexin/wexin_06.png)
![Configuration Page](/images/zh/deploy/bots/wexin/wexin_07.png)

## Start Using

Once logged in, other WeChat users can send messages to your WeChat account to trigger LangBot's automatic replies.
